Inside FableVision

Four tools that do the tedious part.

Each one is opt-in, plainly explained in-game under /client → ❓ How it works, and single-player wherever it could ever affect other players.

press G

Ask AI about your screen

Capture the screen, type a question, and get an answer from the AI you choose — Gemini, GPT, Claude, or Groq (free & fast) — using your own key.

  • The screenshot rides with that one question, then detaches.
  • It can see your inventory too — ask "what should I craft?"
  • It knows the whole mod — ask it how any command works.
/build

Auto-build any schematic

Drop a .schem, .litematic, .schematic or .nbt file in and watch a textured ghost of every block appear where it goes.

  • Every block is paid from one real chest — survival-fair.
  • Build several at once, or one-at-a-time with a stop between.
  • A live materials list turns green when you've got enough.
Create World → ✨ Custom spawn

Find the exact seed you want

Describe a spawn in your own words, or pick a structure and biome. FableVision checks thousands of real seeds a second with the game's own world-gen and drops the winner into your world settings.

  • Exact aims for right where you spawn; Fast picks 100–800 blocks out.
  • "plains next to a cherry grove", "pale garden NOT next to a forest", big/huge biomes.
  • You spawn normally — nothing is placed or moved.

/client

Crystal-PvP vision & quality of life

The little fixes that make fights readable and worlds easier to share.

  • Low fire & shield, fullbright, no explosion particles.
  • AppleSkin-style food HUD; a 10-second auto-leave timer.
  • One-click LAN + Bedrock hosting (with Geyser) via /client lan.

Safe by design

Yours, and only yours.

The whole mod was audited for exactly this: your data stays on your machine.

Your AI key never leaves your PC

It's stored locally, sent only in a request header, and never shown on screen or written to any log. The custom-spawn wish even keeps its own separate key.

Only the AI you pick is ever contacted

Four endpoints exist — one per AI provider — and only the one you chose is used. The seed finder, builder and everything else make zero network calls.

Nothing is spawned in or teleported

The seed finder just finds a real seed with real world-gen — you spawn exactly as vanilla would.

Multiplayer-safe

Building and LAN sharing switch themselves off on servers, so nothing touches a world you don't host.

Install

Drop it in and go.

FableVision is a client-side Fabric mod. You only need it on your own game.

Minecraft 26.1.2 Fabric Loader ≥ 0.19.3 Fabric API Java 25 MIT license
⬇ Download FableVision.jar
  1. Install Fabric Loader for 26.1.2 and add Fabric API to your mods folder.
  2. Drop FableVision.jar into the same mods folder.
  3. Launch, then type /client — or press G to ask AI, and open Create World for a custom spawn.

The Download button serves the mod right from this site. Also coming to Modrinth. Open-source under the MIT license — free to use and modify, just keep the credit.

Pick your version

The Download button follows your pick. 26.2 changed too many client APIs for a straight rebuild and Forge is a different loader — both are coming as their own release.
